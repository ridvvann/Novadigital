FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing and services

What's included in each plan?

Each plan includes different levels of branding, social media management, and support. The Starter plan covers basic needs, Professional adds strategy and more platforms, while Enterprise provides comprehensive brand management with dedicated support.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades will take effect at your next billing cycle. We'll help you transition smoothly between plans.

Do you offer custom packages?

We understand that every business is unique. Contact us to discuss your specific needs, and we'll create a custom package that fits your requirements and budget.

What's your refund policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all new clients. If you're not satisfied with our services within the first 30 days, we'll provide a full refund, no questions asked.

How long are the contracts?

We offer flexible contract terms. Monthly plans have no long-term commitment, while annual plans offer significant savings. You can cancel anytime with 30 days notice.
