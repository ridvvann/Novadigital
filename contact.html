FAQ

Common questions

Quick answers to questions you might have about working with us

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. A basic logo design takes 1-2 weeks, while a complete brand identity can take 4-6 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you work with businesses outside Somalia?

We work with clients globally. Our digital-first approach allows us to collaborate effectively with businesses anywhere in the world through video calls, project management tools, and digital communication.

What's included in your branding packages?

Our branding packages typically include logo design, color palette, typography selection, brand guidelines, business card design, and social media templates. Specific inclusions vary by package level.

Can you help with ongoing social media management?

Yes! We offer comprehensive social media management services including content creation, posting schedules, community management, and performance analytics. We can manage your social presence long-term.
